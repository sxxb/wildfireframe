Wildfire is a web and mobile app that helps you stay safe during Bushfire Season. Wildfire collates important information in one place for you, so you can make important decisions quickly. Wildfire can also keep track of your bushfire preparation and evacuation plans, so you know where to find them when you need them. It is our hope that Wildfire can make a real difference for people living in bushfire prone areas, by making everything from preparing, to monitoring, to evactuating or defending as simple as possible, so you can take action.
